## Macros:
**Calories:** 984kCal - **Carbs:** 112.5g | **Fat:** 19.6g | **Protein:** 84.5g

## Ingredients:

**Dry:**
- 75g of white flour
- 10g of Coconut flour
- 7g of dry yeast
- 15g of baking powder 
- 1g of salt
- 50g of chocolate whey protein
- 30g of 100% cacao powder  

**Wet:**
- 2 eggs
- 1 banana (mashed into a puree)
- 85g of Skyr
- 250g of pumpkin puree 

## Directions: 

1. Mix all the dry ingredients and the wet ingredients in two distinct bowls.
2. Pour the wet ingredients into the dry ones.
3. Mix SLOWLY with a spatula.
4. Once all is mixed up (bubbles should form up) you can cook it at 160°C for 35 minutes.

## Side notes:

You can eat it cold or hot but I personally prefer it cold, or even if you are willing to wait to have it from the fridge.
